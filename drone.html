FPV Drones

First Person View, or FPV, drone racing, is a sport where participants control "drones", equipped with cameras while wearing head-mounted displays showing the live stream camera feed from the drones. Similar to full size air racing, the goal is to complete a set course as quickly as possible.

My drone time line

I first got into this hobby when I bought my first Photography Drone The DJI Spark.

After doing a little research I learn about FPV drones. There were quite a few differences when it came to the FPV drones

For starters these things are fully customizable. All the way down to the type of motherboards and cameras you can install. Another thing that drew my attention was that I would no longer have to look at a screen to fly. I could now buy a VR like headset and fly it as if I was in the pilots seat

But just like picking up any new hobby I was a noob. I had no idea where to even start when it came to building these things. So i bought a prebuilt one called Eachine Wizard X220.

Slowly but surely, Youtube video after youtube video I was able to build my first drone from scratch

After programming the whole thing it was now time to master flying it. I spent many hours in a flight simulator learning. Unlike the DJI Spark this drone did not have a self level or auto hover. Which mean my fingers have to be controlling this thing at all times. With no return to home button available on my custom drone meant I needed to learn to land which sounds easy but its a real challenge depending on where you wanted to land this thing

With a few months of practice this is what I can do
