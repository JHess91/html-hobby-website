Apex Legends

Another one of my favorite games that ive recently picked up is Apex legends. This is a battle royal type game. 20 teams of 3 drop into the map and the last team standing wins. The available play area on the island shrinks over time, forcing players to keep moving or else find themselves outside the play area which can be fatal.

Like CSGO apex legends also has a ranked mode where playeds are rewarded for winning causing them to gain "RP" which when reached to a specific amount allows the player to move up in rank. Losing decreases "RP" which lowers your rank.

Each "legend" has specific skill and ultimate ability that help your team throughout the game.

League Of Legends

And then theres league of legends...

If you like to rage then this game is for you. League of legends aka League aka LoL is MOBA type game (multiplayer online battle arena) In the game, two teams of five players battle in player-versus-player combat, each team occupying and defending their half of the map. Each of the ten players controls a character, known as a "champion", with unique abilities and differing styles of play. During a match, champions become more powerful by collecting experience points, earning gold, and purchasing items to defeat the opposing team. In League's main mode, Summoner's Rift, a team wins by pushing through to the enemy base and destroying their "Nexus", a large structure located within.
